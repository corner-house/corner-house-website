## Hidden Costs Buyers Miss at Krisumi Forest Reserve

The krisumi forest reserve price is available on request. Indicatively, a 3 LDK configuration starts at approximately Rs 6.29 Cr. That number is not your budget. Here is what gets added.

**Preferential Location Charges (PLC):** Higher floors carry a per-sqft PLC premium. Park-facing units carry an additional PLC. Corner units and those with a specific aspect may carry further charges. At Krisumi Forest Reserve, PLC ranges from approximately Rs 150 to Rs 400 per sqft depending on the specific unit. On a 3,000 sqft unit, that adds Rs 4.5 Lakh to Rs 12 Lakh before anything else.

**GST at 5%:** Because Krisumi Forest Reserve is under construction and will remain under construction until 2030, GST at 5% applies on the entire agreement value. On a Rs 6.29 Cr base price, that is approximately Rs 31.5 Lakh.

**Stamp duty and registration:** Haryana charges 7% stamp duty for male buyers and 5% for female buyers on the agreement value, plus 1% registration charges. On a Rs 6.29 Cr agreement, male buyers pay approximately Rs 50.3 Lakh in stamp duty and Rs 6.3 Lakh in registration. Female buyers pay approximately Rs 35.9 Lakh in stamp duty. Joint registration in both names (husband and wife) qualifies for the female stamp duty rate in most cases, consult your legal advisor to confirm.

**Maintenance corpus:** Krisumi typically charges a maintenance corpus deposit covering 24 months of estimated maintenance in advance. Expect approximately Rs 8 to 12 Lakh for a 3 LDK unit.

**Club membership:** Mandatory club membership runs approximately Rs 3 to 5 Lakh per unit.

**Car parking:** Covered parking is typically priced separately from the base unit cost. Expect Rs 5 to 8 Lakh per covered slot. Two-car parking is standard for 4 LDK units.

Add all of these and the all-in cost on a Rs 6.29 Cr base unit lands between Rs 7.4 Cr and Rs 7.8 Cr before any loan-related costs. That 18 to 22% premium above the base price is consistent with what we see across Gurgaon luxury projects, but the exact number at Krisumi Forest Reserve depends on your unit, floor, and facing. Always request the allotment letter with every charge listed before signing any booking form.
