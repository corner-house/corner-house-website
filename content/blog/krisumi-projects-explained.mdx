## The Krisumi City Master Plan ,  Six Phases, One Township

Krisumi City is a 65-acre master-planned group housing colony in Sector 36A, Gurgaon, developed by Krisumi Corporation, the joint venture between Krishna Group and Sumitomo Corporation of Japan. The entire township sits on the Dwarka Expressway corridor, approximately 1 km from the Northern Peripheral Road junction.

The krisumi city sector 36a master plan is structured as six krisumi phases, each with its own HARERA registration, its own tower count, its own unit mix, and its own possession timeline. The three project names ,  Waterfall, Waterside, and Forest Reserve ,  correspond to groups of phases within this master plan.

Think of Krisumi City the way you think of a large residential colony. The colony has one address and one master plan. The individual phases within it are separate RERA-registered projects that happen to share infrastructure, landscaping, and the overall Indo-Japanese design language by Nikken Sekkei.

## Krisumi Waterfall Residences ,  Phase 1, Delivered

Krisumi Waterfall Residences is the first completed phase of Krisumi City. Phase 1 of Krisumi Waterfall Residences has been delivered on time, meeting its RERA-registered possession date. Buyers in this phase have taken possession and the towers are occupied.

This is the single most important data point in the krisumi projects explained story. In a market where multi-year possession delays are treated as normal, Krisumi Corporation delivered Phase 1 of Waterfall Residences on time. That track record is the primary credibility signal for buyers considering the later under-construction phases.

Krisumi Waterfall Residences offered 2 BHK and 3 BHK configurations. The project is now a secondary market asset. If you see listings for Krisumi Waterfall Residences on 99acres or MagicBricks, those are resale units from existing owners, not new bookings from the developer.

**Key facts for Krisumi Waterfall Residences:**
- Status: Delivered (Phase 1 on time)
- Booking: Resale only, no new developer inventory
- Location: Krisumi City, Sector 36A, Gurgaon
- Developer: Krisumi Corporation (Krishna Group and Sumitomo Corporation)

Krisumi Waterside Residences covers Phases 3 and 4 of Krisumi City. This is where the naming confusion most commonly occurs. Buyers searching for krisumi forest reserve vs krisumi waterside residences sometimes land on Waterside listings thinking they are the same project.

## Krisumi Forest Reserve ,  Phases 5 and 6, New Launch

Krisumi Forest Reserve, officially named Krisumi Waterside Residences The Forest Reserve on the HARERA portal, covers Phases 5 and 6 of Krisumi City. This is the newest and most premium phase in the krisumi city sector 36a master plan.

The Forest Reserve is HARERA registered under two separate numbers: RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/944/676 for Phase I (The Forest Reserve) and RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/945/677 for Phase II (The Forest Reserve II). Both registrations are active and current on haryanarera.gov.in.

The krisumi forest reserve vs krisumi waterside residences difference is most visible in three areas. First, the Forest Reserve sits on 6.49 acres with three towers of 30 floors each, achieving a lower density and higher open space ratio than earlier phases. Second, the architecture by Nikken Sekkei applies the full Chinju-no-mori sacred grove concept with 13 specific Japanese design principles embedded in the site layout. Third, the Forest Reserve is priced at a significant premium over Waterside, reflecting both the product evolution and the later position in the master plan when corridor values have matured.

Possession for Krisumi Forest Reserve is November 2030 as per the HARERA registration.

**Key facts for Krisumi Forest Reserve:**
- Status: Under construction, new launch
- HARERA Phase I: RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/944/676
- HARERA Phase II: RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/945/677
- Towers: 3 towers, 30 floors each

## How to Confirm Which Phase a Unit Belongs To

If a broker or seller tells you a unit is in Krisumi Forest Reserve, verify it yourself in two minutes. Go to haryanarera.gov.in and search by the RERA number they provide. The registration must show RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/944/676 or RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/945/677 to be a genuine Forest Reserve unit.

If the RERA number does not match either of those, the unit is in a different Krisumi phase, not the Forest Reserve. This is the most common confusion in the krisumi projects explained journey ,  buyers are shown one project and told it is another.

The HARERA number is the definitive identifier. Not the project name, not the brochure, not what the broker says. The number on the portal is the source of truth.

Krisumi Waterfall Residences and Krisumi Forest Reserve are two different phases within Krisumi City, the 65-acre master township in Sector 36A, Gurgaon. Forest Reserve covers Phases 5 and 6, is under active construction, and carries a HARERA-registered possession date of November 2030. The two projects have different RERA numbers, different configurations, different tower counts, and different price points. Forest Reserve is the most premium phase in the krisumi city sector 36a master plan with 3 LDK, 4 LDK, and Penthouse configurations not available in the earlier phases.

### How many phases does Krisumi City have?

Krisumi City in Sector 36A, Gurgaon is developed across six phases under three project names. Phase 1 is Krisumi Waterfall Residences, now delivered. Phases 3 and 4 are Krisumi Waterside Residences, under construction. Phases 5 and 6 are Krisumi Forest Reserve, the newest launch with HARERA registration numbers RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/944/676 and RC/REP/HARERA/GGM/945/677.
